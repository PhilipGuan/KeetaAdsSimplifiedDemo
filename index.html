The floating slot system ensures ads never dominate the feed — through three safeguards.
1 Scatter Strategy — Consecutive Limit
Max 2 consecutive ad slots. A natural result must break any longer chain.
2 Same-Brand Interval Rule
Two stores from the same brand must be separated by at least 3 positions in the final list.
3 Density Control
Per page (10 stores), ad stores ≤ X (configurable). Currently capped at 4.
